My Backstory

As a small child, I, as many others, was interested in video games, my first ones being Wii Sports and New Super Mario Brothers Wii. When I got a bit older, my parents got me a Playstation 3, where me and my siblings played (and fought about) Minecraft. A bit further in life, I got a Xbox 360 where I played Rollercoaster Tycoon and Tony Hawk Pro Skater 3. And of course I enjoyed (mostly) every moment of it. When I reached the age of about 10, during school, I started to get into Scratch, a block based programming "language" designed to teach kids the basics of programming. I wasn't very good in it, but around 12 I managed to make a (barely functioning) game! Around 14, when I got my own very old pc, I made another game, this time in Gamemaker Studio 2, which was a tower defence game, for a school project. That continued into me making a platforming shadow game, for a game jam, and although it was bad, and unfinished, when I published it, I got comments of people saying that they loved it! At that moment, a fire in my heart went ablaze, that was really the moment I was sure that I wanted to get into Game Development. Then, at 16, I actually did it, I got into a school to learn how to program, the big leagues! And here I am now, making this portfolio, in HTML, I have come very far from the first game I ever created, but this isn't my dream yet. My dream is to make people happy by giving them a good game they can enjoy! Now for some more "professional" information! I am proficient in: - HTML - CSS - C# - C++ - GML (Which is basically Lua) Furthermore, I worked in: - Unity - Gamemaker Studio 2 - Blender (For a project) - Ableton (For music) And lastly, I am "good" in using Git for repositories and project management!
